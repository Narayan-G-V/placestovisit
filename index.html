Welcome to Manali

A mountain town that blends adventure, serenity and unforgettable panoramas. Pack for sunlight by day and cosy wool at night.

Top Places to Visit

Hadimba Temple

A peaceful wooden temple set in cedar forest — perfect for culture & photos.

Solang Valley

Adventure hub for paragliding, skiing and ropeway rides with valley views.

Old Manali & Manu Temple

Wander narrow lanes, cafés and riverside walks; experience local life.

Travel Tips

  1. Carry warm layers.
  2. Prefer local guides.
  3. Respect temple rules.
  4. Stay hydrated on hikes.
  5. Book transport early.
